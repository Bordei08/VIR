VideoTube

About

We want to create a Web application that allows the specification of textual annotations and metadata, plus the expression of opinions regarding videos made by a person or group of people. The system will manage the video productions, including their organization on various criteria: category, year of production, links with other related films, protagonists. Textual and graphic statistics exported in minimally open formats, CSV and SVG will be provided.

For more information you have the site documentation.
